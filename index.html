My name is Phoebe Racz and I am a San Francisco-based design student with an interest in print and web design.

PetalHeads

People and petals go hand in hand. The uniquness of each piece accentuates the beauty of the whole. I wanted to incorperate florals into portraits of disninct human faces in order to brighten the other.




Sue Doeksen:
Personal Logo

When asked to created a personilized logo for the incredible designer, Sue Doeksen, I made sure to incorperate her iconic and cartoonish style, aswell as using her blonde hair and blue eyes to pay tribute to her as a woman, as well as an artist.

Shoe Shell

My task was to create a life-size version of something in wire. Naturally, I chose my favorite shoe.

Lady Vision

Magazines are one of my favorite sources of design inspiration. These are two collages I made in honor of the ways that women and girls find joy in our surronding, and how those aspects can look different.

Eat Your Heart Out

I enjoy baking as a form of expressing love for the people in my life. Typography and lettering is a big aspect of my love for design, even in icing. This was a cake I made for a dear friend, Anastasia. Happy Birthday (everyday).
